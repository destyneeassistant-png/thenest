Study Rhythm You’ll Use Every Week

1. First pass

Read/listen/watch for understanding. No perfect notes. Mark confusing ideas.

2. Feynman explain

Close the book and explain the concept in plain language out loud. If you get stuck, that is the study target.

3. Quiz

Use section quizzes quickly. Use domain quizzes after weekly synthesis. Don’t wait until you feel ready.

4. Repair

Every missed item gets a why-wrong/why-right note and one memory cue.

5. Retrieve

Daily flashcards + weekly mixed review. Memory comes from pulling it out, not rereading.
